珞珈心新2017

A MOOK Book for All the Freshmen in WHU

Category: Book Design

Project Type: Group

Date: August 2017

Role: Editor, Typography, Illustration


Abstract
Luojia Xinxin is a freshman handbook which provides the most comprehensive, authoritative and practical information for every new undergraduate student in Wuhan University. It has been published annually since 2015 and as the design director of Luojia Studio in 2017, I was responsible for the whole preocess of the book.

Content Structure
Luojia Xinin is composed of three parts. They are “Life in Campus”, “Introduction of School-Level Organizations & Clubs” and “Information of Departments”. Contents are all written by students, for instance, the first part is written by Student Union and the second part is written by corresponding organizations.

The amount of pages of the book is 136. Each student will get the customized version of the book that the third part only contains the department or school that the student has enrolled in. However, we would design all the contents of 33 departments in our university, therefore the total amount of pages is over 300.

My Role
I decided the overall style of the book, designed the cover and the layout of the contents and directed the rest of the design. I was also responsible to contact with those who wrote the content.
