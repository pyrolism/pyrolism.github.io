Octopass 八達充

Future Public Wireless Charging Service with Indoor Positioning

Category: Entrepreneurship Project

Project Type: Group

Date: June 2018 - August 2018

Role: Define, Research, Product Design, Service Design, IxD Design


Abstract
Octopass is a conceptual wireless charging service that specificly designed for people’s mobile devices. It contains a novel technology, a peripheral device designed for smartphones and a service designed for public places.

Motivation
People have suffered from the battery inadequacy issue. Our devices have improved over the years, but the way we charge them stays the same. In the future with IoT, 5G and more widely available wearable devices, the problem will become more vital.
